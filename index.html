WHAT: We are going to Miramar Beach in Destin, Florida. It's a Family Affair!

 

 

WHO:  The primary target audience is all of the First Cousins, but you can also bring your families.

 

WHEN:  July 25 - July 28, 2024

 

COST:  The NON REFUNDABLE Registration Fee is $150.00.  A deposit of $100 per person is Due by October 15, 2023…. And the $50 balance is due by January 15, 2024.
Remember, All fees are Non-Refundable. 

 

If you find that you are unable to attend after you pay, you are welcome to transfer this credit to another family member, but there are no refunds.

 

If you choose to pay the $100 Deposit vs the FULL $150 Registration Fee, the remaining balance is due by January 15, 2024.

 

DETAILS:

This fee will cover:

·      1 Family Excursion
